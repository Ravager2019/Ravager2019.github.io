Welcome To The UNLV IT Workforce Pipeline's Website!

This program aims to provide a year-long immersive and accelerated learning for high school junior and senior students who have some IT experience to pursue further education and employment in IT fields. The program maps out training against the national IT Competency Model led by the Employment and Training Administration (ETA) 7 and includes preparation in three tiers: foundational competencies that focus on employability skills that are generally needed in the workplace, technical competencies that are core to the IT industry allowing workers to move easily across industry sub-sectors (fluidity), and occupation- related competencies that tie to specific jobs which will be obtained through internship. We recruited 30 Grade 11 & 12 students for the 2023-2024 cohort.
