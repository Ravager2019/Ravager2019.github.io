Program Information

Program Description:

Students will take a blend of online and in-person learning sessions that includes two college courses CS135 (computer science) -3 credits in fall; EDW135 (employability skill) -3 credits in spring), three IT workshop series (i.e., Introduction to Cybersecurity, Introduction to Cloud Computing, and Web Design and Internet Computing), and industry certification exam series prepared by guest speaker Upon completion, student will earn one of the following industry- recognized credentials: AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner, CompTIA IT Fundamentals (ITF+), CompTIA A+, CompTIA Cloud+, CompTIA Network+, or CompTIA Security+. Credentials are stackable and college course credits will count toward a bachelor’s degree program such as BS in Computer Science, Information Technology, or

Summer paid internship - The industry partners will pay for up to 10 weeks of summer internship at $15+ per hour and guarantee job interviews for all students who complete the program

Program Benefits:

Paid tuition, registration & certification exam fees Paid full-time summer internship with employers Technical & employability skills training Company visits, career fair, & certification exam preparation Peer & professional mentors Guaranteed job interview for program completers

Nevada high school students will earn up to SIX (6) UNLV credits (CS135 & EDW135) and ONE (1) IT certification of your choice; CCSD students will earn additional ONE (1) CCSD high school credit* (Earned credits are based on successful completion of the program including the college courses, internship and passing IT exams)
